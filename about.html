About

Founded by Paul Henry Laffin, Print For Good Films creates authentic, cross-cultural short documentaries that inspire social and environmental change worldwide. Based in Toronto, we are dedicated to powerful, impactful storytelling.

Mission

To deliver authentic, cross-cultural narratives that not only captivate global audiences but also foster meaningful cultural exchange and inspire positive social change.

Vision

To be a global leader in short documentary filmmaking, creating impactful, award-winning films that inspire audiences worldwide. We aim to deepen cultural collaboration, support emerging storytellers, and champion environmental conservation through innovative film and digital platforms.

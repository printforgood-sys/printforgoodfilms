The Problem

Your best people are spending hours every week on tasks that AI could handle in seconds — summarizing documents, formatting reports, answering the same questions, scheduling, managing inboxes. Every hour lost there is an hour not spent on the work that actually grows your business.

Most businesses know AI can help. Few know where to start, and fewer still have someone who can actually build anything. That is where I come in.

What I Do

I spend one to two weeks inside your business — observing, asking questions, and identifying the specific tasks where AI can free up time. Then I build something: a working tool, an automated workflow, a prompt system your team can use immediately.

You receive a written AI Opportunity Map — a plain-English document outlining every opportunity I found, every recommendation I make, and every tool I built. Yours to keep, regardless of what you decide next.

The Guarantee

You do not pay a cent unless you are genuinely satisfied with what I deliver. This is a no-risk engagement. If you do not see the value, we shake hands and part ways. Simple as that.
